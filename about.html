A Message from the Founder

I started Urban Threads in 2024 because I was tired of "fast fashion" that fell apart after two washes. I wanted to build a shop that offered pieces you'd actually want to keep for years.

Today, we work directly with small-scale manufacturers to ensure every stitch meets our standards. We aren't a big corporation—we're a small team dedicated to helping you look and feel your best.
